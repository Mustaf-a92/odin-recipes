Chicken with Noodles

Noodles are great, but they have almost 0 healthy value, what about is we raise this number?

Now, we'll move into junk food, but we will try to make it as healthier, and more delicious as possible. We won't use the prepaired oil with it, instead we'll make our own recipe now, and of course we will use the noodles spices, because they woud do a great job in enhancing the taste, and of course you can avoid them if you want.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Packs of Noodles.
  • 1 Lemon.
  • 1Tbs of tomatoe paste.
  • 1 chicken breast (small if possible).
  • 1 Onion.
  • 1 green pepper.
  • 1Tbs of oil.

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into small cubes.
  2. Cut the onion into small cubes.
  3. Cut the green pepepr into small cubes.
  4. Heat the pan for few seconds, then add the oil and lower the heat.
  5. Add the chicken breast, and let it cook and stir it until it changes color.
  6. Then, add the onion, the green pepper, and stir again until the chicken changes color again.
  7. Now, while the chicken is taking the onion's flavour, put the Noodles in another cooking pot until it cooks.
  8. Put the tomatoe paste spoon into the chicken pan, and stir again, and then cut the lemon in half and squeeze it in the chicken pan.
  9. When the Noodles are ready, add them to the chicke pan, and stir them together.
  10. Add the food into a cooking dish, and there you go!
