Kabsa

Kabsa's Recipe and why would you choose to cook a Kabsa

Kabsa is a famous dish almost in every Khaliji country, due to its a bit spicy flavour, and being rice. I mean, every Khaliji person lovers rice, so yeah.

Even though it might seem like a really complicated dish to make, it is quite simple! and the results are very impressing, just stick with us!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of tomatoe paste
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 big onion softly chopped
  • 4 cups of hot water
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1.5KG of chicken, 8-10 pieces
  • 2 pieces of clove
  • 6 pieces of garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup of mashed tomatoe
  • 400 grams of fresh chopped tomatoe

Steps:

  1. melt the butter in a big cooking pot.
  2. Add the chicken, the garlic, and the onion and stir until the onion cooks and the chicken takes a brown color.
  3. Add the mashed tomatoe, and let it cook on low fire for a few minutes.
  4. Add the tomatoe paste, the cloves, and the salt and black pepper.
  5. Cook the mix for a few minutes.
  6. Add the water and let the mix boil, and then lower the heat and cover the pot.
  7. Cook the mix for 30 minutes.
  8. Add the rice into the pot and stir well.
  9. Cover the pot again.
  10. Let the mix cook on low heat for 35 - 40 minutes.
  11. Cook the mix well until the rice is cooked.
  12. Put the rice on a large serving dish, and there you go!
